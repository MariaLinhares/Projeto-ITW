Beijing 2008

The 2008 Summer Olympics, officially the Games of the XXIX Olympiad and also known as Beijing 2008, were an international multisport event held from 8 to 24 August 2008, in Beijing, China.

Vancouver 2010

The 2010 Winter Olympics, officially known as the XXI Olympic Winter Games and also known as Vancouver 2010, were an international winter multi-sport event held from February 12 to 28, 2010 in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, with some events held in the surrounding suburbs of Richmond, West Vancouver and the University of British Columbia, and in the nearby resort town of Whistler.

Rio de Janeiro 2016

The 2016 Summer Olympics, officially the Games of the XXXI Olympiad and also known as Rio 2016, was an international multi-sport event held from 5 to 21 August 2016 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Tokio 2020

The 2020 Summer Olympics, officially the Games of the XXXII Olympiad and also known as Tokyo 2020, was an international multi-sport event held from 23 July to 8 August 2021 in Tokyo, Japan.

Significance

The Olympic Games are a major international multi-sport event featuring summer and winter games, in which thousands of athletes from around the world participate in a variety of competitions. The Olympic Games have a long and rich history dating back to ancient Greece, where the first Olympic Games are believed to have been held in 776 BC.

Origin

The ancient Olympic Games were held in honor of Zeus, the king of the gods in Greek mythology, and were held every four years at the sacred site of Olympia. The Games were a religious festival as well as a sporting event, and featured a variety of athletic competitions such as foot races, long jump, shot put, and chariot races. The ancient Olympics were also a time of peace, as a sacred truce was declared during the Games, and all wars and conflicts were put on hold.

Nowadays

The modern Olympic Games were founded by Pierre de Coubertin, a French educator, who sought to promote international understanding and peace through sport. The first modern Olympic Games were held in Athens, Greece in 1896, and featured 280 athletes from 13 countries competing in 43 events.
